# Ethiopian Immigration and Citizenship Service (ICS): Contact & Locations

This guide contains the official operational windows, primary locations, and digital portals for the Ethiopian Immigration and Citizenship Service (ICS).

---

## 1. Official Websites & Digital Portals

The ICS operates specific online portals depending on whether you are managing local applications or global diaspora requests:

* **Main Informational Site:** [ics.gov.et](https://ics.gov.et)
* **Local Passport Application Portal:** [ethiopianpassportservices.gov.et](https://www.ethiopianpassportservices.gov.et)
* **Diaspora / Overseas Platform:** [digitalinvea.com](https://digitalinvea.com)

---

## 2. Primary Office Locations (Addis Ababa)

While there are multiple regional branch offices throughout Ethiopia (such as in Hawassa, Adama, Dire Dawa, Bahir Dar, Mekelle, and Gondar), the main service hubs are located in the capital:

* **ICS Headquarters (Gotera):**
  * **Location:** Located near the Gotera Interchange, Kirkos Sub-city, Addis Ababa.
* **Foreigner’s and Consular Services Department:**
  * **Location:** Located in the Arada Sub-city (near Piazza / Black Lion area), Addis Ababa.

---

## 3. Working Hours

To accommodate high demand, the ICS has transitioned major passport operations to extended service hours across its key centers (including the Gotera and Tikur Anbessa/Black Lion branches) and select regional offices:

* **Monday through Saturday:** 8:30 AM – 10:00 PM *(Extended night shifts are dedicated primarily to passport enrollment and distribution processing).*
* **Sundays:** 8:30 AM – 6:00 PM.

> **Note:** Standard administrative departments and non-passport services typically adhere to standard government shifts (8:30 AM – 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday).
