# Ethiopian Passport Service Fees, Payment Methods, and Refund Policy

## I. Passport Service Fees (ETB)
Fees are calculated based on the service speed and the specific condition of your application (e.g., lost vs. standard renewal).

### 1. Standard Fee
* **New Passport:** 5,000 ETB *(issued within 5 to 10 business days)*
* **Standard Renewal:** 5,000 ETB
* **Renewal with Information Correction:** 12,500 ETB *(e.g., name, photo, or date of birth updates)*

### 2. Urgent / Fast-Track Fees
Expedited options incur a significant premium and require proof of urgent travel:
* **Urgent Service (5 Days):** 20,000 ETB total
* **Fast-Track Service (2 Days):** 25,000 ETB total

### 3. Replacement Fees
Replacing lost or damaged valid documents requires additional administrative verification:
* **Standard Lost Passport Replacement:** 13,000 ETB *(Regular processing time)*
* **Urgent Lost Passport Replacement:**
    * **5-Day Urgent:** 28,000 ETB
    * **2-Day Fast-Track:** 33,000 ETB
* **Lost Passport Replacement with Corrections:** 20,500 ETB *(Standard)* / up to 40,500 ETB *(2-Day Fast-Track)*
* **Standard Damaged Passport Replacement:** 13,000 ETB

### 4. Child Passport Fee
Minors under 18 pay the same baseline fees as adults for local passport issuances:
* **Standard Delivery:** 5,000 ETB
* **5-Day Urgent:** 20,000 ETB
* **2-Day Fast-Track:** 25,000 ETB

> **Diaspora Note:** For applications submitted from abroad (via embassies/consulates), child passport fees are set at **100 USD** for standard or **200 USD** for urgent 2-day processing.

---

## II. Payment Methods
All fees must be settled digitally. Once you complete your application on the ePass portal, you have a **3-hour window** to complete the transaction.

* **Local Payments:** Settled through integrated electronic payment pathways, including **Telebirr**, **CBE Birr**, and commercial mobile banking apps.
* **Diaspora Payments:** Settled online using credit or debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) via the **Digital Invea** portal.

---

## III. Refund Policy

### Non-Refundable Policy
Passport fees paid to the Ethiopian Immigration and Citizenship Service (ICS) are **strictly non-refundable**. 
If you cancel your appointment, fail to show up, or have your application rejected due to insufficient or fraudulent documentation, the government will not refund the processed fee.

### Double Payments
If a system glitch causes you to be charged twice for a single application code, you must visit the main ICS headquarters in Addis Ababa with bank transaction receipts to submit a manual correction and refund claim.
