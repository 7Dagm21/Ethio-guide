# Passport Payment Guide: Process, Methods, Receipts, & Confirmation

This guide outlines the complete payment workflow, accepted methods, and confirmation steps required by the Ethiopian Immigration and Citizenship Service (ICS) to secure your passport appointment.

---

## 1. How the Payment Process Works

The payment process acts as a bridge between submitting your digital application and securing your in-person biometric slot:

1. **Generate Reference Number:** Complete your passport application online on the official portal or Digital Invea app to generate a unique **Payment Reference Number / Order Code**.
2. **Execute Payment:** Use your selected digital payment channel (mobile app or USSD code) to enter this Reference Number.
3. **Authorize:** Review the displayed transaction details, input your secure PIN, and authorize the fee.
4. **The 3-Hour Lock:** Your payment must be successfully executed **within 3 hours** of submission. If unpaid within this window, the order expires, your chosen appointment slot is released, and you must reapply.

---

## 2. Accepted Payment Methods

The ICS accepts digital payments only. Physical cash is not accepted at the service counters.

### Local Payments (Within Ethiopia)
* **Telebirr:**
  * **App:** Navigate to *Utility Payments* -> *Government Services* -> *Immigration and Citizenship Service*.
  * **USSD:** Dial `*127#` and follow the prompts.
* **Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE):**
  * **App:** Use the **CBE Birr** app or **CBE Mobile Banking** app. Navigate to *Government Services* -> *ePassport Services* -> enter your *Order Code*.
  * **USSD:** Dial `*889#`.
* **Other Digital Banking Options:** Integrated local commercial bank apps utilizing the **Derash** shared payment platform or **Flygate** (for online portal payments).

### Diaspora & Overseas Applications
* **International Cards:** Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) are processed securely directly through the **Digital Invea** web portal or mobile app.

---

## 3. Receipt Requirements

Once your payment goes through, the transaction acts as official proof of your registration:

* **Digital Receipt:** You will receive an immediate SMS confirmation containing the transaction ID and your payment order status.
* **Hard Copy Requirement:** You must log back into the ePass portal, download your digital payment receipt, and **print a physical copy**. 
* **Appointment Verification:** You must bring this physical printout to your biometric appointment alongside your original documents. Without it, the counter agents cannot initiate your physical file.

---

## 4. How Payment is Confirmed

Your payment confirmation happens in real time across the integrated systems:

* **Portal Status Change:** As soon as the transaction is finalized via CBE Birr, Telebirr, or card, your portal application status will automatically update from **"Pending Payment"** to **"Paid / Ready for Appointment"**.
* **Downloadable Confirmation Document:** Once confirmed, the portal activates your scheduling page to choose your appointment date. 
* **Gate Pass:** After selecting your slot, the system generates a downloadable *Immigration Service Payment Confirmation* document featuring your official application barcodes, appointment date, and payment receipt metadata. **This document is your mandatory gate-pass to enter the ICS facility.**
