# Biometric Photo Requirements & Specifications

This document outlines the strict guidelines for uploading your digital passport photo. Photos that do not meet these international standards will be systematically rejected by the ICS automated system.

---

## 1. Background
* **Requirement:** The background must be completely plain, solid white.
* **Examples of Rejections:** Photos taken against off-white, blue, grey, or textured walls will be systematically rejected. There must be no patterns, objects, or other people visible in the frame, and no shadows behind the head or on the background.

---

## 2. Size & Composition
* **Requirement:** The physical dimensions must be exactly 30 x 40 mm (standard Ethiopian passport/custom digital frame ratio). 
* **Examples of Rejections:** Standard landscape images or square photos (like US 2 x 2 inches) will not fit the digital rendering portal. The head must occupy 70–80% of the vertical frame, measured from the bottom of the chin to the top of the hair.

---

## 3. Expression
* **Requirement:** A strictly neutral facial expression is required.
* **Examples of Rejections:** Smiling, frowning, smirking, raised eyebrows, or squinting are forbidden. The mouth must be completely closed, and eyes must look directly at the camera lens with a natural, wide-open gaze.

---

## 4. Glasses
* **Requirement:** Eyeglasses are generally not permitted in the photograph.
* **Examples of Rejections:** Wearing sunglasses, tinted lenses, or daily prescription glasses that cause glare or frame reflections over the eyes will result in immediate rejection. If glasses are medically mandatory and cannot be removed, the lenses must be completely clear, and a signed doctor's medical note must accompany the application.

---

## 5. Headwear
* **Requirement:** Head coverings, hats, caps, or headbands are prohibited.
* **Examples of Rejections:** Casual wear like baseball caps, beanies, or fashion headbands are not allowed.
* **Exception:** Head coverings worn daily for established religious purposes (such as a hijab or traditional netela) are permitted, provided the fabric does not cast shadows and is pulled back far enough to expose the entire face from the bottom of the chin to the top of the forehead, leaving both edges of the face completely visible.

---

## 6. Lighting and Quality
* **Requirement:** Uniform, bright lighting must be used across the entire face.
* **Examples of Rejections:** Photos with red-eye, harsh flash reflections, low-resolution pixelation, or heavy shadows on one side of the face will be denied. The image must show natural skin tones, maintain sharp focus, and be completely unaltered by digital beauty filters or smoothing software.
