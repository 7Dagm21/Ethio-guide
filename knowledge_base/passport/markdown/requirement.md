# Comprehensive Guide to Ethiopian Passport Services

This guide provides detailed information on the processes, requirements, fees, and administrative procedures of the Ethiopian Immigration and Citizenship Service (ICS) as of 2026.

---

## 1. How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

All initial passport applications must begin online. While you fill out the application and pay online, **you must still attend an in-person appointment** at your chosen ICS branch to submit biometric data (fingerprints and passport photo).

1. **Register & Log In:** Visit the official portal at [ethiopianpassportservices.gov.et](https://www.ethiopianpassportservices.gov.et) or use the **Digital Invea** mobile app. Create an account using a valid email and verify it.
2. **Fill the Form & Upload Documents:** Select **"New Passport Application"**. Fill in your personal details exactly as they appear on your official government IDs. Scan and upload clear JPEG files of your required documents (birth certificate, Kebele ID, etc.) and a digital passport-sized photo with a solid white background.
3. **Select Location & Book Appointment:** Choose your preferred ICS delivery site (such as the Main Office, Piazza Branch, or regional offices). Select an available date and time slot for your biometric appointment.
4. **Make the Payment:** Pay the application fee digitally via CBE Birr, Telebirr, or other integrated banking systems. Keep the digital receipt and print out your **appointment confirmation page**. You must complete the payment within 3 hours of booking.
5. **In-Person Biometrics:** Go to your selected ICS office on your appointment day. Bring your printed appointment confirmation, original renewed Kebele ID, and original birth certificate. They will capture your fingerprints and take your official digital photo.

## 2. Passport Types, Speed, and Fees

The cost of your passport is determined by the processing speed you select. All newly issued passports are secure **e-Passports** containing biometric chips.

* **Regular Service (5 to 10 business days):** 5,000 ETB
* **Urgent Service (5 business days):** 20,000 ETB
* **Fast-Track Service (2 business days):** 25,000 ETB

### Criteria for Urgent / Fast-Track Services
To prevent misuse, the ICS requires applicants to upload supporting documents proving their immediate need for travel, such as:
* An official medical referral letter for treatment abroad.
* An active scholarship letter, university admission, or DV lottery selection.
* A valid emergency business invitation or foreign residence permit.

## 3. General Frequently Asked Questions (Q&A)

### Can I renew after expiration?
**Yes.** You can renew your passport after it has officially expired. There are no late renewal penalty fees, but you will not be able to book international travel or clear customs until your new passport is processed, printed, and collected.

### Can I travel with an expired passport?
**No.** Airline boarding rules and international immigration laws require a valid passport with at least six months of remaining validity before your travel date.

### How long does processing take?
* Standard Service: 5 to 10 business days from your biometric appointment.
* Urgent Service: 5 business days.
* Fast-Track Service: 2 business days.
* Diaspora Service: 2 to 4 weeks if applying from an overseas embassy or consulate.

### Can someone else collect my passport?
**Only under strict legal exceptions.** Normally, you must collect your passport in person. However, someone else may collect it on your behalf only if they present an officially authenticated **Power of Attorney (Wukilna)**, your original physical application/payment receipt, and their own valid National ID (Fayda) or passport.

### Can I apply abroad?
**Yes.** If you are living outside of Ethiopia, you can apply online via the **Digital Invea** platform (website or mobile app) and complete your biometrics at the nearest designated Ethiopian Embassy or Consulate.

### Can I change my appointment?
**Yes, but only under specific limits.** You can log back into your portal account before your scheduled date to reschedule your biometrics appointment. You cannot reschedule once your original appointment slot has already passed.

### Can I cancel my application?
**You can stop the process, but you cannot get a refund.** While you can choose not to attend your biometrics appointment, the fees paid to the ICS are **strictly non-refundable**.

### How long is the passport valid?
* **Ages 25 and above:** 10 years.
* **Ages under 25 (including minors):** 5 years.
