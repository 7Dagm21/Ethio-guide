# Passport Biometric Appointment: Booking, Rescheduling, & Requirements

This document outlines the operational guidelines for booking, changing, or attending an in-person passport appointment with the Ethiopian Immigration and Citizenship Service (ICS).

---

## 1. Booking

Your appointment cannot be booked in isolation; it is directly generated by completing a paid application.

* **How it Works:** Once you fill out your application and upload your documents on the official [ethiopianpassportservices.gov.et](https://www.ethiopianpassportservices.gov.et) portal or the **Digital Invea** app, you are prompted to select an ICS branch location and choose an open date and time slot from the scheduling calendar.
* **The Payment Lock:** The booking is **not reserved** until you complete your digital payment via Telebirr, CBE Birr, or your mobile banking app. You must pay **within 3 hours** of submitting the form; otherwise, your selected slot is released, and you must restart the booking process.
* **Finalizing:** Once paid, download and print the **appointment confirmation page** (which includes your application number, appointment date, time, and barcodes).

---

## 2. Rescheduling

If you cannot make your original date, you can change your appointment online, but only under certain conditions:

* **How to Reschedule:** Log back into your account on the ePass/Digital Invea portal. Go to *"My Workspace/Applications"*, select your active application, and click on **"Reschedule Appointment"**. Choose a new available date and time slot, then save the changes.
* **Print New Confirmation:** You must download and print the *new* appointment confirmation slip reflecting the updated date.
* **The Threshold Rule:** You must reschedule **before your appointment date has passed**. If you miss your scheduled appointment slot entirely, you cannot reschedule it online. You will either have to visit the main office to seek an manual override or forfeit the application (and fee) and start over.

---

## 3. Cancelling

* **No Refunds:** You can choose to cancel your application or simply not show up for your appointment, but all passport fees paid to the ICS are **strictly non-refundable**.
* **System Inactivity:** If you cancel your booking or do not show up, your digital file will remain inactive in the system. To apply again in the future, you will have to create a new application request and pay the fees again.

---

## 4. Arrival Time

Punctuality and crowd control are highly enforced at physical ICS offices due to high volume.

* **When to Arrive:** You should arrive at your selected ICS office **30 to 45 minutes before** your scheduled time slot.
* **Lateness Consequence:** If you are late by more than 30 minutes past your scheduled slot, security and ticketing systems may deny you entry, causing you to miss your appointment.
* **Prohibited Items:** To ensure a smooth security screening, do not carry large bags, laptops, or recording equipment into the biometrics queue.

---

## 5. Required Documents (For the Biometric Appointment)

When you walk into the ICS branch, you must bring the exact **physical originals** of the documents you scanned and uploaded during the online booking phase. **Photocopies alone are not accepted.**

### For a New Passport
* **Printed Appointment Confirmation Page** (with the barcodes visible).
* **Printed Payment Receipt** (showing your reference/order code).
* **Original Renewed Kebele ID** (or a temporary Kebele ID accompanied by an official supporting letter from your local Kebele administration office).
* **Original Authenticated Birth Certificate** (mandatory for anyone under 40 years old).
* **National ID Card (Fayda)** or your physical Fayda slip showing your National ID number.

### For a Passport Renewal
* **Your Original Current Passport** (plus clear physical photocopies of the main bio-data page).
* **Printed Appointment Confirmation & Payment Receipt** from the portal.
* **Original Renewed Kebele ID** and your **Fayda ID**.

### For a Lost Passport Replacement
* **Original Stamped Police Report** (must explicitly state your name and details of the lost passport).
* **A Photocopy of the Lost Passport** (if you have one).
* **Original Renewed Kebele ID** and **Fayda ID**.
* **Printed Appointment Confirmation & Payment Receipt** (reflecting the 13,000 ETB lost replacement fee).

### For a Child Passport (Under 18)
* **Original Authenticated Birth Certificate** of the minor.
* **Original Renewed Kebele ID or Passport** of the Parent(s) or legal guardian.
* **Signed Parental Consent Form** (printed from the portal).
* **Original Court Guardianship Documents** (only if the applicant is a legal guardian rather than a biological parent).
* **Printed Appointment Confirmation & Payment Receipt**.
